Site FAQ

  • I tried downloading a game... What is this weird "7z"/"gz"/"xz" thing????
    • To save space and bandwidth, a lot of the downloadable files on this site are compressed! If you're on Windows, you should be able to install 7zip or another archive manager to extract it. Most Linux distributions should be able to handle compressed files automagically. If you're on mac I really have no clue. That was your own doing.
  • What's your policy on generative AI?
    • I will never use genAI on this website, except for possible demonstration purposes. In this case, the usage will be made excessively clear. I value the fact that everything on this site was made by me and reflects who I am, instead of the will of The Machine. While current genAI systems are certainly interesting and have legitimate uses, 99% of the things they're being used for are dumb as hell (ignoring the vast other problems with the technology)
  • What about cookies? Javascript?
    • This site currently does not use any form of Javascript or cookies. This may change in the future, as those would be cool to learn, but this site will always work without them. And, of course, if I do get around to learning Javascript, it will mostly likely be all code I wrote myself. No external libraries, and definitely no tracking.
